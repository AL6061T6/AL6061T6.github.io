Why you do sewing?

I expected mechanical engineering would help me to get a mediocre life which is my favorite subject, and that was why I decided to major in mechanical engineering.
I got job as a robot research associate after graduation and there's not really any problem in living except I start to want to do what I really like for a living.

What else can I do for a living?

I've done lots of fanarts related with Japanese anime culture, drawing and sewing are the one of them.
I really loved drawing, but because I was terrible at it, it was not very effective to learn or be good at it.
At least I thought I was decent at sewing, so It would be a sewing which could give profits, rather than a drawing.

That why I've done sewing project so one.

Why you like SmartDoll?

I've been big fan of making doll's clothing quite a long time, and also made some outfits for my first doll Jasmine, Blue Fairy. But I wanted to get a new one to let sewing project get traction, so I searched lots of doll I could get.
Of course I was intrested in DD(Dolfie Dream), and Iris Collect. But the one which impress me the most was SmartDoll.

It was not just beacuse that doll was kawaii but, rather the story about how it's made.

Although I don't know whe Danny choo is at all(designer of SmartDoll), the articles about how and why SmartDoll is made really blew my mind.

That was just overwhelming to me.
Could someone build his comapny and make a signature doll just because he wants his own company and loves his chracter?
It sounded like insane and felt so jealous. Because I convinced that only SmartDoll is the only one that can give this kind of energetic, and I decided to get one(Mirai, Suenaga).

At least what I can do

Still I don't know what kind of things could gives me reasonable protifs for a living. Koreans work too much, and just earning a livng takes quite much time for me.
But, I cannot just do nothing.
Because I thought I need to let more people know about me first, I started to make outifts of Mirai with my all of skills I got from previous fanart works, and do social media.
I know I might not make it as much as another person had done,but I just hope I could also find my way to make sewing as part of my earnings.
