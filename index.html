Why you do sewing?

When I was a high school student, I expected mechanical engineering, could give me a mediocre life. It was one of the most famous majors by which can get a job, and because my favorite subject was physics, I decided to major in mechanical engineering.
Despite the pandemic, I got a job as a robot research associate after graduation and that job gave me a quite decent living as I expected except I start to want to do what I really like for a living.

What else can I do for a living?

I've done lots of fanarts related with Japanese animetic characters and, drawing and sewing are the one of them.
I really loved drawing but, I was terrible at it, It was not that effective to spend time for training.
At least I was decent at sewing, so I thought It would be sewing which could give profits, rather than a drawing.

That's why I've done sewing projects so on.

Why you like SmartDoll?

I've been a big fan of making doll's clothing for quite a long time. My first doll was Jasmine of "Blue Fairy" and I did lots of drills to make clothes with her. But to make my sewing project get traction, I wanted to get a new one. So I started to search for lots of dolls I could get as a new one.
Of course, I was interested in other dolls like DD(Dolfie Dream), Iris Collect, etc. But the one which impress me the most was SmartDoll.

It was not just because that doll was kawaii but, rather the story about how it's made.

Although I didn't know who Danny choo(designer of SmartDoll) is at all, the articles about why and how SmartDoll was made blew my mind. That was just overwhelming to me.
It just simply didn't make any sense to me that someone built his own company and made a signature doll from scratch while quitting his job at one of the global top companies.
It just sounded insane and felt so jealous. I'm convinced that the doll which can give me this kind of energetic story is SmartDoll only and that's why I like SmartDoll and I bought it.

At least what I can do

Still, I don't know what kind of things could give me reasonable profits for a living. Just earning a living takes quite a time in my case.
But, I cannot just do nothing.
Because I thought I need to let more people know about me first, I started to make outfits of Mirai with all of my skills I got from previous works, and do social media.
I know I might not make it as much as another person had done, but I just hope I could also find my way to make sewing a part of my earnings.
