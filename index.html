Why you do sewing?

When I was a high school studuent, I expected mechanical engineering, could give me a mediocre life. It was one of the most famous major by which can get a job, and because my favorite subject was physics, I decided to major in mechanical engineering.
Despite pandemic, I got job as a robot research associate after graduation and that job gave me a quite decent life as I expected except I start to want to do what I really like for a living.

What else can I do for a living?

I've done lots of fanarts related with Japanese animetic characters and, drawing and sewing are the one of them.
I really loved drawing but, I was terrible at it, It was not that effective to spend time to train.
At least I was decent at sewing, so I thought It would be a sewing which could give profits, rather than a drawing.

That why I've done sewing project so on.

Why you like SmartDoll?

I've been a big fan of making doll's clothing quite a long time. My first doll was Jasmine of "Blue Fairy" and I did lots of drill to make clothes with her. But to make my sewing project get traction, I wanted to get a new one. So I strated to search lots of doll I could get as a new one.
Of course I was intrested in other dolls like DD(Dolfie Dream), or Iris Collect, etc. But the one which impress me the most was SmartDoll.

It was not just beacuse that doll was kawaii but, rather the story about how it's made.

Although I don't know who Danny choo(designer of SmartDoll) is at all, the articles about why and how SmartDoll is made blew my mind.

That was just overwhelming to me.
Not anyone can build his own comapny and make a signature doll from scratch just because he want it.
It sounded like insane and felt so jealous. I convinced that SmartDoll is the only one that can give this kind of energetic story, so I made my decesion.
